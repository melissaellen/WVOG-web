Helpful Information

Payment of membership fees:

• Make sure your current information is on file: Name, address, email address, current phone numbers, all unit numbers that you own.
• Place your unit number on each check each month.
• Place your check in an envelope before placing it in the assessment box. The boxes are not waterproof and checks have been ruined due to rain.
• Make sure your payment gets into the assessment box before the end of the day on the 15th of each month in order to avoid getting late fees assessed.
• If you are behind on your assessments, please call Q3 Realty Partners to setup a formal payment plan.
• Mailing labels will be available if you are one of the few who mail in assessments. Please call Q3 Realty Partners and they will have them available upon request.

Trash Pickup

Thursday morning.
• Garbage cans can be out after 4pm on Wednesday and must be moved back inside by 10pm Thursday.
• Residential collection may be delayed due to the following holidays: Memorial Day, July 4, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day and New Years Day. As a general rule, holidays push the residential pickup schedules back one day with regular schedules resuming the following week.

Dogs

Must remain on a leash at all times while in the Village. Do not leave your dog tied up outside alone. They should only be restrained outside of your condo if you are present with your pet. Pick up after your pet. Currently we do not supply poop bags so please bring your own with you.

Town of Chesterton

Pool

There is no lifeguard on duty. Pool is for residents use only. Must be 14 or older to swim without a parent or guardian. Pool is generally open Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day weekend. Pool tags are available from Barb Wood, 219-929-5556.

Vehicles

All cars belonging to residents and parked in the Village are mandated to have a car sticker. If your car doesn't have a sticker, please contact Barb Wood, 219-929-5556. Please do not park, or have others park, on the street.

Play Areas

Please teach your children to respect Village property and grounds. There is a playground in the Village near the entrance off of 23rd Street, two basketball hoops, and grassy areas behind the condos and between the streets. Please do not allow them to climb the trees or fences, or bounce their balls against the condo walls or garages.

Garage Sale
